Presentations:

Inclusive design for accessible products and services - Molly Watt and Chris Bush

According to the Office for National Statistics, there are over 10 million disabled people in the UK, representing around 15% of the population. More than ever before, the need to deliver inclusive, accessible digital products and services constitutes both a moral and a commercial imperative. This talk will set a meaningful context, demonstrate good and bad examples and give you useful tools and processes to start embedding inclusive design and testing across your teams.

 

Our hosts Sigma will be providing complimentary soft drinks, beer, and pizza.

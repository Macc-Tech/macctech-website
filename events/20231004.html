Tree of Light + Fix The Six!

Wednesday, 4th October 2023

Nexer Digital
The Ropewalks, Newton Street, Macclesfield, SK11 6QJ

18:00 - 21:00

Note: Past Event

Historic Links: Meetup

We're back for another Macc Tech evening, hosted by Nexer Digital.

Join us for a series of talks and a chance to meet, mingle and share ideas, with refreshments provided.

 

Presentations:

Fix The Six - Danny Lancaster

A Deep Dive into Common Accessibility Challenges and DIY Testing

How accessible is your website? Are you inadvertently turning away potential clients and customers?

For this talk, we will delve into the realm of web accessibility, shedding light on how it can be a game-changing opportunity for expanding your audience by fostering inclusivity in your apps, websites, and communications.

Accessibility is more than tech for good; it's a fundamental aspect of ensuring that your digital products and services cater to everyone who relies on them. It stands as both a legal requirement for some, and a moral and business imperative for all.

 

Tree of Light - Nick Young

The tech behind the Tree of Light, the Macc Town Christmas Tree lights in 2020.

A look at the interesting mix of tech used including cellular, light and sound as well as the CoViD (un)friendly UX, and the iterative approach.

 

Our hosts Nexer Digital will be providing complimentary refreshments and catering.

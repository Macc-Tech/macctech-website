Blockchain, and Blocked - bring a plunger!

Monday, 18th September 2017

The Park Tavern
158 Park Lane, Macclesfield, SK11 6UB

19:30 - 22:30

Note: Past Event

Historic Links: Meetup

This month, we are back to our usual format of two talks.

 

Presentations:

Blockchain - Stephen Webb

Blockchain is the Technology behind Bitcoin, Ethereum and other Cryptocurrencies - but it's potential reaches far beyond simple commerce. This is an introduction to the concept and a look at the wide-ranging effect it promises to have on the world.

 

Blocked.org.uk - Tom Chiverton

A developer by day, by night Tom volunteers with the Open Rights Group to protect and promote your rights and security where the on and off line worlds meet. Tonight you can help rescue local businesses that are being removed from the Internet. It could be yours!
